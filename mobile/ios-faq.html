Why use this app?

The Volusion iOS App gives you quick access on your devices to manage your store!

  • Create new products and edit current products.
  • Review and manage orders from payment to shipment, including Fraud Scores.
  • View customer details, including billing and shipping addresses and order history.
  • View at-a-glance updates on orders, sales revenue, top products and search terms.
  • Check Volusion notifications and low inventory alerts.

What do I need to use this app?

You must have a Volusion Admin account to use this app. Your account must be designated as an Admin.

How do I sign in?

You can log into one or more stores through the app. On the login screen, tap each line to enter your store name (domain name), user account name (email), and your password. The app uses a smart keyboard with symbols, letters, and numbers as needed. An option displays to enabled Touch Id, allowing registered users to login. Repeat the process to log into all of your stores, adding them to a selectable list.

How do I sign out?

Tap the More tab. Tap Log Out in the top right.

What does the Dashboard display?

View all sales initiated and completed through your storefront and earned revenue. Tap to see at-a-glance charts for Revenue and Orders with larger graphics to see detailed amounts per the last 24 hours, 7 days, and 90 days. You can review a revolving carousel of Top Products purchased through your storefront, tappable to view and edit product details. The final section displays the Top Searches (10 keywords searched) performed on your storefront, helping you plan SEO and keywords for products.
